⚙️ Risk Engine Methodology

This application utilizes a two-stage **Linear Regression pipeline**. In the first stage, market price forecasts determine the baseline €/m² valuation. In the second stage, your friend's Linear Regression Cost Predictor maps geometric features (GFA, rooms) and inflation parameters directly to the final construction cost.

To establish the risk envelope, the system uses the model's Mean Absolute Error (MAE = €30,882.89) to parameterize a normal error distribution: $N(\mu = \text{Predicted Cost}, \sigma = 1.2533 \times \text{MAE})$. The 90% Confidence Interval and the cumulative distribution S-curve are computed analytically, offering high-performance risk modeling.
